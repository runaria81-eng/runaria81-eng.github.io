Steam Privacy Policy

Call the Cat | Last updated: July 12, 2026

This policy covers the Steam version of Call the Cat. Mobile versions may use separate mobile privacy policies.

1. About This Policy

Call the Cat is a casual cat mini-game developed by Baren81. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the Steam version of the game.

2. Information We Process

The Steam version does not require users to create a Baren81 account. Baren81 does not directly request a user's name, email address, phone number, postal address, contacts, photos, or precise location.

The game may store gameplay progress, settings, scores, achievements, unlock states, and similar game data locally on the user's device.

3. Steam Platform Services

When Steam features are used, Valve may process Steam account identifiers, Steam ID, ownership status, play activity, achievements, cloud save data, overlay browser activity, purchase and refund information, diagnostics, and other platform information according to Valve's own policies.

For more information, please review Valve's privacy policy at https://store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement/.

4. Analytics and Advertising

The Steam version is not intended to use mobile advertising SDKs such as AdMob. It is also not intended to show rewarded mobile advertisements.

Platform-level analytics or diagnostics may be provided by Steam or the operating system according to their own settings and policies.

5. Purchases and Refunds

Purchases and refunds for the Steam version are handled through Steam. Baren81 does not directly collect or store payment-card or bank-account information.

6. Purposes of Processing

Information described in this Policy may be processed to provide and maintain game functionality, save and restore progress, support Steam platform features, process purchases or refunds through Steam, detect technical problems, and improve game reliability.

7. Sharing and International Processing

Information may be processed by Valve, Steam, operating-system providers, or service providers used to distribute and operate the game. These providers may process information on servers located outside the user's country or region, subject to their privacy policies and applicable safeguards.

Baren81 does not sell users' names or contact information.

8. Retention, Deletion, and User Choices

Local game data remains on the device until it is deleted, reset, or the game is removed. Steam Cloud, achievements, purchase records, and platform data are retained according to Steam account settings and Valve's retention policies.

Users may manage Steam-related data and privacy choices through Steam account settings where available.

9. Children's Privacy

The game is intended for a general audience. Baren81 does not knowingly ask children to provide names, contact details, or other directly identifying information. If you believe that a child has provided personal information directly to Baren81, please contact us so that we can review and, where appropriate, delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the game's features, service providers, platform requirements, or legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted at the same location with a revised last updated date.

11. Contact

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact Baren81 at rurununky@naver.com.
