Call the Cat

Call the Cat is a casual cat mini-game developed by Baren81. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the app.

Information We Collect

The app itself does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or address.

Advertising

This app may use Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect device information, advertising identifiers, usage data, and other data necessary to provide, measure, and improve advertising services.

For more information about how Google handles data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

This app may offer in-app purchases. Payment processing is handled by the app store platform, such as Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not directly collect or store payment information.

Game Data

The app may save game progress, item counts, settings, and similar gameplay data on the user's device. On supported platforms, cloud save features may be used to restore game progress.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
