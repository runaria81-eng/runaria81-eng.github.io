Privacy Policy

Call the Cat | Last updated: July 12, 2026

This policy covers the Steam, Google Play, Apple App Store, and mobile versions of Call the Cat, as long as the listed platform features and third-party services are used in that version.

1. About This Policy

Call the Cat is a casual cat mini-game developed by Baren81. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app and why it is processed.

2. Information We Process

The app does not require users to create a Baren81 account. Baren81 does not directly request a user's name, email address, phone number, postal address, contacts, photos, or precise location.

The app and its third-party service providers may automatically process the following categories of information:

  • Device and app identifiers, such as an app instance ID or device-level identifier;
  • Approximate location inferred from information such as an IP address;
  • Product interaction and usage data, such as app launches, gameplay events, feature use, and ad interactions;
  • Advertising data used to provide, limit, measure, and improve advertisements;
  • Purchase history information, such as a product identifier and purchase result, but not payment-card information; and
  • Diagnostics, such as crash, performance, and other technical diagnostic information.

3. Analytics

The app may use Google Firebase Analytics or platform analytics to understand how the app is used and to improve gameplay, stability, and features.

Analytics may process device and app identifiers, app version and device information, approximate location, product interactions, gameplay events, purchase results, advertising interactions, and diagnostic information. Analytics data is not used by Baren81 to identify a user by name.

For more information, please visit Firebase Privacy and Security and Google Privacy Policy.

4. Rewarded Advertising

The mobile version may use Google AdMob to provide optional rewarded advertisements. Rewarded ads are shown only after the user chooses to request an advertisement.

AdMob may process device information, advertising identifiers where permitted, approximate location, product interaction, advertising data, and diagnostic information to provide, prevent fraud in, limit, measure, and improve advertising services.

Depending on the user's region, consent choices, and platform settings, AdMob may provide personalized, non-personalized, or limited advertisements. Declining personalized advertising does not prevent the app from providing non-personalized or limited advertisements where available.

For more information, please visit Google Advertising Policy.

5. Purchases and Platform Services

The app may offer purchases through Steam, Google Play, or the Apple App Store. Payment processing is handled by the relevant platform provider. Baren81 does not directly collect or store payment-card or bank-account information.

The app may process a product identifier, transaction status, and purchase result to deliver or restore purchased content, prevent duplicate rewards, provide support, and measure app functionality.

6. Game and Cloud Save Data

The app stores game progress, item counts, settings, and similar gameplay data on the user's device.

On supported platforms, cloud save services provided through Steam, Google Play Games, Apple Game Center, iCloud, or similar platform services may process this data so that game progress can be restored. These platform services are governed by their own privacy policies and the user's platform account settings.

7. Purposes of Processing

Information described in this Policy may be processed to provide and maintain app functionality and cloud saves, deliver optional rewarded advertising, measure app usage and feature performance, process and restore purchases, detect fraud or technical problems, and improve the app and service reliability.

8. Sharing and International Processing

Information may be processed by service providers used by the app, including Google, Firebase, AdMob, Apple, Steam, and app-store platform services. These providers may process information on servers located outside the user's country or region, subject to their privacy policies and applicable safeguards.

Baren81 does not sell users' names or contact information.

9. Retention, Deletion, and User Choices

Local game data remains on the device until it is deleted, reset, or the app is removed. Cloud-save, analytics, advertising, and purchase records are retained according to the settings and retention policies of the relevant platform or service provider.

Users may manage advertising and privacy choices through any consent form shown in the app and through their device, Apple, Google, Steam, or advertising settings. Some information controlled solely by a platform provider must be managed through that provider's account or privacy controls.

10. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for a general audience. Baren81 does not knowingly ask children to provide names, contact details, or other directly identifying information. If you believe that a child has provided personal information directly to Baren81, please contact us so that we can review and, where appropriate, delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app's features, service providers, or legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted at the same location with a revised last updated date.

12. Contact

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact Baren81 at rurununky@naver.com.
