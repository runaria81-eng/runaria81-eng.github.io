Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

1. About This Policy

Call the Cat is a casual cat mini-game developed by Baren81. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the app, why it is processed, and the choices available to you.

2. Information We Process

The app does not require users to create a Baren81 account, and Baren81 does not directly request a user's name, email address, phone number, postal address, contacts, photos, or precise location.

The app and its third-party service providers may automatically process the following categories of information:

  • Device and app identifiers, such as an app instance ID or device-level identifier;
  • Approximate location inferred from information such as an IP address;
  • Product interaction and usage data, such as app launches, gameplay events, feature use, and ad interactions;
  • Advertising data used to provide, limit, measure, and improve advertisements;
  • Purchase history information, such as a product identifier and purchase result, but not payment-card information; and
  • Diagnostics, such as crash, performance, and other technical diagnostic information.

3. Firebase Analytics

The app uses Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and to improve gameplay, stability, and features. Firebase Analytics may process device and app identifiers, app version and device information, approximate location, product interactions, gameplay events, purchase results, advertising interactions, and diagnostic information.

The app does not send a user's name, email address, phone number, postal address, or payment-card information to Firebase Analytics. Analytics data is not used by Baren81 to identify a user by name.

For more information, see Firebase Privacy and Security and the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Rewarded Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to provide optional rewarded advertisements. Rewarded ads are shown only after the user chooses to request an ad. AdMob may process device information, advertising identifiers where permitted, approximate location, product interaction, advertising data, and diagnostic information to provide, prevent fraud in, limit, measure, and improve advertising services.

Depending on the user's region, consent choices, and platform settings, AdMob may provide personalized, non-personalized, or limited ads. Declining personalized advertising does not prevent the app from providing non-personalized or limited ads where available.

For more information, see the Google Advertising Policies and Technologies and the Google Privacy Policy.

5. In-App Purchases

The app may offer in-app purchases. Payment processing is handled by the Apple App Store or Google Play. Baren81 does not directly collect or store payment-card or bank-account information. The app may process a product identifier, transaction status, and purchase result to deliver or restore purchased content, prevent duplicate rewards, provide support, and measure app functionality.

6. Game and Cloud Save Data

The app stores game progress, item counts, settings, and similar gameplay data on the user's device. On supported platforms, cloud save services provided through Google Play Games or Apple Game Center may process this data so that game progress can be restored. Those platform services are governed by Google’s or Apple’s privacy policies and the user's platform account settings.
